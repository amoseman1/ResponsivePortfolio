About ME!

Hi everybody! I'm Amy, even though auto-correct constantly wants to make my name "any". I'm originally from Bloomington, IN. After college, I moved to Key Largo, FL and lived the island life for 5 years. I've been in Colorado and the Salida/Buena Vista area since 2014. I just bought a home here, so am I a local yet? I've spent most of my life in the hospitality industry working in bars and restaurants. I love meeting people and having a unique work experience everyday, it will be a challenging transition to a computer oriented job. I used to be a raft guide but have transitioned into a ACA certified whitewater instructor, and work with a non-profit called ElevateHer that educates middle school girls through outdoor education. My full time job is working in the tap room of a brewery in Poncha Springs, CO, called Elevation Beer Company. When I'm not being a work-aholic, my partner and our new pup, Loki are playing in the mountains. I love to whitewater raft, whitewater SUP, mountain bike, hike, snowboard, back-country ski, and go scuba-diving (not in Colorado though, I have to visit the ocean every couple months to get my fix of sand and saltwater). I also love dabbling in photography and cooking all the recipes I can get my hands on!
